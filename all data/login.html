    Questions?


    What is NXT?
    NXT (pronouced "next") is a new generation crypto currency launched in the last quarter of 2013 designed to overcome many of the limitations of Bitcoin. In this sense NXT is considered a 2.0 crypto currency and offers many new features such as green mining (called transparent forging), one minute blocks and many built-in features such as the alias system, distributed asset exchange and account control. Visit www.nxt.org for more information.
    Why should I use the MyXEL.info Online Wallet?
    NXT is a peer-to-peer network and participants are encouraged to download and install a compatible stand alone client (many options are available at www.nxt.org). However, as with Bitcoin many users would like to access their funds from different locations and devices such as the work computer, a mobile device, etc. This is where the MyXEL.info Online wallet comes in handy, as you can easily and quickly access funds you have deposited in our service without having to download and install anything

    The second point worth noting is that MyXEL.info Online Wallet has a strong focus on simplicity and ease of use to attract new users to NXT. We only make available NXT services that are relevant to the majority of users. In this sense, some of the core functionality of NXT may not be initially available in the MyXEL.info Online Wallet until we idenfity a relevant use case for it.
    How does the MyXEL.info Wallet protect my NXT? Is it secure?
    When you register in MyXEL.info, you choose two passwords. The first is used to login into the website (login password) and the the second is used to encrypt your wallet (master password). We do not have access to your wallet funds, the only way to access your funds is by decrypting the wallet and the master password is required for that. In addition to this, we also encrypt all wallets a second time using a site-wide encryption key. This is to make it even more unlikely that an attacker can get access to your funds.

    That being said, we also need to stress that as with any online service, no matter how much we invest in security nothing is 100% secure. You should take precautions, don't re-use the same password in other sites, keep your passwords in a safe place, and if you have a lot of NXT, spread the risk by keeping only a portion of it in the online wallet and the rest in a few other wallets.
    Can MyXEL.info access my funds?
    No! When you register, you choose a master password. This password is used to encrypt your wallet and you are the only person that can access it using the master password. We have no access to your account or your funds.
